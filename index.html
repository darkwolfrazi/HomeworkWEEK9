Personal Bio

I've been drawing for all of 22 years of most my life. I'd like to say that each one on my painting tells a story about me. Maybe what I might be going through, how I'm feeling during a certain time of year or day, or even of a few amazing placed that i found. In each of my painting I try to give off the message that "This is me" and I'd like to help other capture that feeling. I do not consider myself as an expert artist but my work has gained the attention of many followers across social media and I've managed to put together a platform that i wanted to take to the next level, this is that attempt. I specialize in 3 main forms of art styles, Anime, nature, and I dabble in Tattoo designs. I can do special orders and I charge by the Hour and Size and pricing is open to negotiation, especially for clients that are loyal customers. Don't forget to follow my social media pages, both links on the contact page, and welcome to Winters Wonderland.
